Built for everyone

Works for every person in the building.

Students, teachers, admins, and parents — outPass is designed to make everyone's day run smoother.

Request a pass in seconds
Enter your name, pick your teacher and destination. No raising your hand, no interrupting class. Your teacher is notified instantly.
Approved with a live timer
Once approved, your pass shows a count-up timer so everyone knows exactly how long you have been out.
See your full history
Review every pass you have used — destination, date, and duration. A clear record that builds accountability naturally.
Return reminders
If you have been out too long, a gentle reminder nudges you back before your teacher has to track you down.
One-tap approve or deny
Requests arrive on your screen with the student's name and destination. Approve or deny without pausing your lesson.
Live hall view
See every student currently out, where they went, and how long their timer has been running — all from one screen.
Blackout times and limits
Lock passes during tests, restrict one student out at a time, or cap daily totals per student. You stay in control.
Usage patterns
Spot trends over time — who leaves often and when. Useful context for parent conversations and student check-ins.
School-wide visibility
One dashboard. Every student currently out of class across the entire building. Know exactly who is where at any moment.
Replaces paper sign-outs
Searchable, exportable digital records for every single pass. No more binders, loose slips, or illegible handwriting.
Emergency-ready
During a drill or lockdown, pull up every student's location instantly. A full timestamped trail for every movement.
Full policy control
Configure destinations, set school-wide pass limits, manage teacher permissions. outPass adapts to your rules.
Weekly summaries
Optional weekly digest: how many passes your child used, where they went, and how long they were out.
Full transparency
No more guessing why your child was late back to class. outPass gives you an honest, verifiable record.
Better conversations
Parent-teacher meetings grounded in shared data — everyone using the same record, not conflicting memories.
Spike alerts (optional)
Opt in to be notified if your child's pass usage spikes — an early, low-pressure signal worth paying attention to.

How it works

Up and running this afternoon.

No IT ticket. No lengthy setup. Most schools are live before the bell rings.

01
School sets up in minutes
Import your roster via Google Classroom, Clever, or CSV. Add teachers, set destinations and pass limits. Done in under an hour — no IT department required.
02
Students enter their name and request
From any device, students type their name, pick their teacher, and tap a destination. No dropdown for estimated time — just quick, honest information.
03
Teachers approve in one tap
A notification arrives with the student's name and destination. One tap to approve or deny. The pass activates with a live count-up timer, and everything is logged automatically.
04
Admins see the full picture
A live school-wide dashboard, daily summaries, and exportable records — all named and timestamped. Paper sign-out sheets are gone for good.
