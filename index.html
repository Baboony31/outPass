Everything your school needs

Outpass gives teachers, staff, and administrators complete visibility into student movement — in real time.

Digital Hall Passes

Replace paper passes with instant digital ones. Students request, teachers approve, and everyone stays informed — all from any device.

Real-Time Monitoring

See exactly who's in the hallway, where they're going, and for how long — with a live dashboard that updates instantly, every time.

Movement Analytics

How it works

Simple for everyone

Outpass is designed to require zero training. If you can tap a button, you can use Outpass.

1

Student requests a pass

Students open Outpass on any device, select their destination, and submit. The pass is automatically approved based on school rules — no interruption to the class.

2

Teacher monitors & controls

Teachers see all active passes in their classroom in real time. If something looks off — too long, wrong destination — they can cancel the pass instantly with one tap.

3

Staff monitor activity

Admin staff get a bird's-eye view of all movement across the school. Safety alerts trigger automatically for unusual patterns — keeping every student accounted for.
