How it works

Up and running
before the bell rings.

No IT ticket. No lengthy setup. Most schools are fully live before the end of first period on day one.

01
School creates an account
Sign up, enter your school name, choose your role. Teachers add their rooms and configure pass limits. Done in under 10 minutes — no IT department required.
02
Students sign out at the kiosk
Any shared device becomes the kiosk. Students type their name, pick their class, tap a destination. The screen resets in 2 seconds so the next student can go.
03
Teachers approve in one tap
The request appears on the teacher's dashboard instantly. One tap to approve or deny. The pass activates with a live timer — everything logged automatically.
04
Students sign back in & review
When returning, students type their name and end the pass. A one-tap feedback rating goes straight to the teacher and admin dashboards.
